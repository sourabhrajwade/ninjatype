# <i class="material-icons">email</i> Contact

Have questions, feedback, or suggestions? We'd love to hear from you!

## Get in Touch

### GitHub
The best way to reach us is through GitHub:
- **Issues**: Report bugs or request features
- **Discussions**: Ask questions or share ideas
- **Pull Requests**: Contribute to the project

Visit the [NinjaType GitHub repository](https://github.com/VivekGupta137/ninjatype) to get started.

### Social Media

Connect with us on:
- **Twitter/X**: Follow for updates and announcements
- **Discord**: Join our community to chat with other users
- **GitHub**: Star the repo and follow the project

## Feedback

Your feedback helps make NinjaType better! Whether you have:
- Feature requests
- Bug reports
- General suggestions
- Questions about usage

We welcome all input and do our best to respond as quickly as possible.

## Business Inquiries

For business-related inquiries, partnership opportunities, or other professional matters, please reach out through GitHub or the project's social media channels.

---

**Response Time**: We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours, though it may take longer during busy periods. Thank you for your patience!
